DMCA Copyright Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

LegalAI respects the intellectual property rights of others and expects our users to do the same. This DMCA (Digital Millennium Copyright Act) Policy outlines our procedures for handling copyright infringement claims.

Copyright Policy

It is our policy to:

  • Respond promptly to valid DMCA takedown notices
  • Remove or disable access to allegedly infringing material
  • Terminate accounts of repeat infringers
  • Respect the rights of copyright owners and users alike

Document Upload Responsibility

When using LegalAI, users are responsible for ensuring they have the right to upload and analyze documents. Do not upload:

  • Copyrighted documents without permission
  • Proprietary legal agreements belonging to others
  • Documents obtained without authorization
  • Materials that infringe on third-party rights

Filing a DMCA Takedown Notice

If you believe that content on LegalAI infringes your copyright, please provide our DMCA Agent with the following information:

Required Information:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work: Describe the work you believe has been infringed, or if multiple works, a representative list.
  2. Identification of the infringing material: Provide specific location information (URL, description) of where the allegedly infringing material is located on our service.
  3. Contact information: Include your name, address, telephone number, and email address.
  4. Good faith statement: Include a statement that you have a good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner, its agent, or the law.
  5. Accuracy statement: Include a statement that the information in your notice is accurate and, under penalty of perjury, that you are the copyright owner or authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner.
  6. Signature: Provide your physical or electronic signature.

Send DMCA Notices to:

Counter-Notification Process

If you believe your content was removed in error, you may file a counter-notification containing:

  • Your contact information (name, address, phone, email)
  • Identification of the removed material and its location
  • A statement under penalty of perjury that you have a good faith belief the material was removed due to mistake or misidentification
  • A statement consenting to jurisdiction of federal court in your district
  • Your physical or electronic signature

Repeat Infringer Policy

LegalAI will terminate user access for repeat copyright infringers in appropriate circumstances. We maintain a record of DMCA notices and may take action against users who repeatedly upload infringing content.

Safe Harbor Provisions

LegalAI qualifies for safe harbor protection under the DMCA as we:

  • Do not have actual knowledge of infringing activity
  • Act expeditiously to remove infringing material when notified
  • Do not receive financial benefit directly attributable to infringing activity
  • Have designated a DMCA agent to receive notices

False Claims Warning

Important: Making false DMCA claims may result in liability for damages, including costs and attorney fees. Only file a DMCA notice if you have a good faith belief that the use is infringing.

Our Response Process

When we receive a valid DMCA notice, we will:

  1. Review the notice for completeness
  2. Remove or disable access to the allegedly infringing content
  3. Notify the user who uploaded the content (if applicable)
  4. Document the takedown for our records
  5. Respond to the copyright owner confirming action taken

Limitations

Please note:

  • We do not store user documents permanently
  • Most content is processed temporarily and then deleted
  • DMCA notices should be filed promptly after discovering infringement
  • We cannot remove content that has already been automatically deleted

Educational Use

LegalAI is designed for educational and informational purposes. Users analyzing documents for educational purposes may be covered by fair use provisions, but this must be determined on a case-by-case basis.

International Copyright

We respect copyright laws of all jurisdictions. International copyright holders may file DMCA notices, and we will respond according to applicable international treaties and agreements.
