Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

Introduction

At LegalAI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our AI-powered legal document analysis service.

Information We Collect

Document Data

  • Legal documents you upload or paste for analysis
  • Text content extracted from your documents
  • Analysis results and AI-generated explanations

Usage Information

  • IP address and browser information
  • Pages visited and time spent on our site
  • Device and operating system information
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Document Analysis: To provide AI-powered legal document analysis and explanations
  • Service Improvement: To improve our AI models and user experience
  • Analytics: To understand how our service is used and optimize performance
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations

Document Processing and Storage

Important: We do not permanently store your uploaded documents or their content. Here's how we handle your documents:

  • Documents are processed in real-time for analysis
  • Document content is temporarily held in memory during processing
  • All document data is automatically purged after analysis completion
  • No copies of your documents are retained on our servers

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL/TLS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure processing environments
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication systems

Third-Party Services

We use third-party services to provide our functionality:

  • AI APIs: For document analysis and language processing
  • Analytics: To understand website usage patterns
  • Advertising: To display relevant advertisements
  • CDNs: For faster content delivery

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Advertising and marketing purposes
  • User preferences and settings

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request information about data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal data

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@legalai-analyzer.com
